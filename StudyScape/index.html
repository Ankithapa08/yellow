    What is StudyScape?

    A platform for students to manage their class notes, integrated schedules, and task tracking using to-do lists.

    1 - Store your class notes

    Access your notes anytime, based on your class schedule and tasks.

    2 - Integrated with Google Calendar

    Never miss a class or event with Google Calendar integration.

    3 - Track your tasks

    Manage and monitor your progress with easy-to-use to-do lists.
